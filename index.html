INTRODUCTION OF US—

PVPIT – A Legacy of Excellence

Padmabhooshan Vasantraodada Patil Institute of Technology (PVPIT), Budhgaon, Sangli, has a storied history and a commitment to providing high-quality technical and engineering education. Established in 1983 by the visionary leader, the late Padmabhooshan Vasantraodada Patil, who served as the Chief Minister of Maharashtra and the Governor of Rajasthan, PVPIT continues to uphold his legacy of excellence.

Located just 10 km from the vibrant city of Sangli, our institute is dedicated to fostering a nurturing and innovative educational environment. Our mission is to serve both the industry and society through creative educational programs and pioneering research initiatives.

Our Vision

  • To become a leading institute in providing high quality technical & engineering education to the aspirants and serve the industry and society through excellent educational programmes, creativity and research.

Diploma

A diploma course is a short-term academic program that focuses on practical and technical skills... A diploma course is a short-term academic program that focuses on practical and technical skills in a specific field. Diploma courses are generally offered after completing 10th or 12th grade and have a duration ranging from six months to three years. They are available in various fields such as engineering, computer applications, fashion design, interior design, hotel management, and medical lab technology. Unlike degree programs, diploma courses are more skill-based and career-focused,They are also more affordable and require less time, while still opening doors to further education or specialized careers.

Degree

A degree course is a long-term academic program offered by universities and colleges... A degree course is a long-term academic program offered by universities and colleges.These courses are typically pursued after completing higher secondary education (12th grade) and have a duration of three to five years, depending on the program. Degree courses include undergraduate programs such as Bachelor of Arts (BA), Bachelor of Science (BSc), Bachelor of Commerce (BCom), and Bachelor of Technology (BTech), as well as postgraduate degrees like Master of Arts (MA) or Master of Science (MSc). They are essential for students who wish to pursue advanced studies, research, or professional careers in fields such as science, engineering, business, arts, education, and more...

Postgraduate

A postgraduate course is an advanced level of academic study pursued after completing an undergraduate degree... A postgraduate course is an advanced level of academic study pursued after completing an undergraduate degree. Postgraduate programs typically include Master’s degrees such as MA (Master of Arts), MSc (Master of Science), MCom (Master of Commerce), MBA (Master of Business Administration), and MTech (Master of Technology). The duration of most postgraduate courses ranges from one to two years. These programs help students enhance their academic qualifications, improve job prospects, and prepare for research or doctoral studies (PhD).

* Admission Process *

PVPIT Budhgaon Application Process 2025

PVPIT offers these kinds of courses through a structured admission process, ensuring a smooth experience for applicants. The Admission Cell assists with document verification and fee payment, providing support to students throughout the process.

  • Register on https://fe2023.mahacet.org/ (B.E./B.Tech/Diploma) or the GATE portal (M.E./M.Tech).
  • Complete the application form with your academic details.
  • Upload scanned copies of the required documents (listed below).
  • Pay the application fee online.
  • Appear for MHT-CET/JEE Main (B.E./B.Tech), GATE (M.E./M.Tech), or submit 10th marks (Diploma).
  • Participate in CAP counselling.
  • Verify documents and pay fees at PVPIT.

Documents Required:

  • 10th/12th marksheet (UG/Diploma) or B.E./B.Tech mark sheet (PG)
  • MHT-CET/JEE Main/GATE scorecard
  • School Leaving Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Aadhaar card
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Income certificate (for scholarships)
  • 4 passport-size photographs
